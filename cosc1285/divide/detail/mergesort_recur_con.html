Merge Sort Algorithm

  • At the first level, we spend cn time
  • At the second level, we spend cn/2 on each subproblem, for a total of cn time.
  • At level j of the recursion, the number of problems has doubled j times. Each problem costs n/2j . Therefore, level j contributes at most 2j (cn/2j ) = cn time.
  • Summing across all the levels (of which there are log2 n), we get a total running time of Î˜(n log n).
