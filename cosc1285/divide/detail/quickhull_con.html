QuickHull

  • ￼1 Sort all points in increasing order of x and then y.
  • 2 Choose the leftmost and rightmost point, which are a chord of the convex hull. (The points are extremal points). Call these points a and b.
  • 3 Separate the remaining points into two sets S1 and S2. All points above ab are in S1 and all below are in S2.
  • 4 Find the point c in S1 which is farthest from ab.
  • 5 Discard all points inside the triangle △abc.
  • 6 Put all points outside of ac in set S1′ .
  • 7 Put all points outside of bc in set S2′ .
  • 8 Run recursively on ac and bc.
  • 9 Abort when the subset contains only the two endpoints of the current chord.
