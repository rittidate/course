BST Traversal

  • Given a (pointer to a) tree, systematically process every node in that tree.
  • For binary trees, we have two links for each node, and therefore we have three basic orders in which we can process the tree.
  • Preorder: The root is visited before the left and right subtrees are visited (in that order).
  • Inorder: The root is visited after visiting its left subtree but before visiting the right subtree.
  • Postorder: The root is visited after visiting after visiting the left and right subtrees (in that order).
