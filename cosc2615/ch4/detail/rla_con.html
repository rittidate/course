Responsibility, Liability, and Accountability

Moral Responsibility Legal Liability Accountability
Attributes of blame (or praise) to individuals. Does not attribute blame or fault to those held liable. Does not necessarily attribute blame (in a moral sense).
Usually attributed to individuals rather than "collectivities" or groups. Typically applies in the case of corporations and property owners. Can apply to individuals, groups of individuals, and corporations.
Notions of guilt and shame apply, but no legal punishment or compensation need result. Compensation can be required even when responsibility in a formal sense is not admitted. Someone or some group is answerable (i.e., it goes beyond mere liability).
